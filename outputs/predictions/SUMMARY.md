1. **Elo ratings** computed from all results since 1872 using tournament-weighted K-factors.
2. **Dixon-Coles model** fitted on 2015–2026 international matches with time-decay weighting (xi=0.003).
3. **All WC matches treated as neutral venue** (except USA/Canada/Mexico host teams get 50% home boost).
4. **Red cards always predicted as 0** — modal value is 0 in ~78% of WC matches.
5. **Playoff slots** (undetermined qualifiers) use median Elo/FIFA points of likely candidates.
6. **Knockout simulation** uses 50,000 Monte Carlo iterations with Dixon-Coles score sampling.
7. **Best-3rd rules**: 8 of 12 third-placed teams advance, ranked by pts→GD→GF→discipline.
